who are elani?

Elani is a kenyan afro pop band consisting of three members;Wambui Ngugi,Maureen Kungu and Brian Chweya.

Since their debut to the music industry in 2013 with the song "jana usiku" they have successful in the music career not only to be nominated in the channen O music video Awards as most Gifted-East but also win an award at All Africa Music Award (AFRIMA) as best African pop band
