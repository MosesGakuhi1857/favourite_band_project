Who are Elani?

Elani is a kenyan afro pop band from nairobi consisting of three members;Wambui Ngugi,Maureen Kungu and Brian Chweya.

Since their debut to the music industry in november 2013 with the song "jana usiku" they have been able to maintain a successful music career to-date.Not only have they been able to be nominated in the channel O music video Awards as most Gifted-East but also win an award at All Africa Music Award (AFRIMA) as best African pop band
