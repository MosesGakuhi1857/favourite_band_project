Who are Elani?

Elani is a Kenyan Afro Pop band from Nairobi consisting of three members;Wambui Ngugi,Maureen Kungu and Brian Chweya. Since their debut to the music industry in November 2013 with the song "Jana Usiku"they have been able to maintain a distinct music career to-date.Because not only have they been able to be nominated in the channel O music video Awards as most Gifted-East but also win an award at All Africa Music Award (AFRIMA) as best African pop band.My Favourite albums are;barua ya Dunia,colours of love,elani instrumentals,Digital Me,Ayin,Speaking in Cursive.Their taste and style of music makes them indifferent which i guess makes them my favourite band.

    Band Members;

  1. 1.Wambui Ngugi

    Vocalist(2008-to-date).She is a singer and a song writer and an Acturial Science gratuate from University of Nairobi and she is very passionate in her christian beliefs.She claims to have a taste for natural stuffs.

  2. 2.Maureen Kunga

    Vocalist(2008-to-date).She is a singer,a song writer and a lawyer.She is 31 years.Her passion for music started while she was in church as she was a member of church choir.

  3. 3.Brian Chweya

    Vocalist,guitarist(2008-to-date),song-writer,singer and a Advocate of the High Court of Kenya.He has a mellow voice and admits to love her mom with passion.
